How to play

1. Find the answer. Read the multiplication question in the left sidebar and steer Pac-Man to the matching number.

2. Choose your lane. Numbers sit in the maze lanes. Run over a number to activate it, or steer around it if it is not your answer.

3. Learn from feedback. Correct answers glow green. Wrong answers glow red and the correct answer glows white before the next question appears, unless Second Chance keeps the current question open for another attempt.

4. Watch the ghosts. Avoid ghosts, or run over a rotating blue orb to frighten them temporarily.

5. Level up. Every configured number of correct answers unlocks another power-up: teleporters, super strength, Radar, Shield, Ghost Freeze, Decoy Pac-Man, Time Warp, Second Chance, Pac-Man Dash, and Ghost Bomb. Pickups are consumed when used; portals remain reusable.

Controls: Arrow keys/WASD or swipe to steer · P to pause
